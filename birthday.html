My Letter to You

My love, on the day the universe decided to bring you into this world, it gave me — eventually — the most extraordinary gift I have ever known: you.

I think about who I was before you, and it feels like reading a book with pages missing. You walked in and suddenly every sentence made sense. You made the quiet parts of me feel seen, and the loud parts feel understood.

Loving you is the easiest thing I have ever done, and the most important. You are the person I want beside me on the ordinary Tuesdays, not just the extraordinary days. You are the home I did not know I had been looking for.

Happy Birthday, my person. Thank you for existing. Thank you for choosing me. Today, every star above you is a wish I am making for your happiness.

— Yours, always ♡

I promise to keep growing alongside you, to give you the space to become whoever you are meant to be, and to love every version of you that comes next.

This is not a birthday message. This is a life-long promise from me to you. ♡
